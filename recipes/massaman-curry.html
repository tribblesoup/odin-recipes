Massaman Curry

A creamy Thai style curry with vegetables and chicken in a peanut flavored sauce.

This curry is a sweet medley of peanuts, carrots, and potatoes cooked to perfection with chicken and flavored with tamarind and coconut milk. It is ready to serve in under an hour. Serve over plain white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 tablespoons curry paste
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 (3/4 inch thick) slice of ginger, minced
  • 1 1/4 pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast meat - cubed
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter
  • 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 3 cups cubed potatoes
  • 2 cups sliced and quartered carrots
  • 3 cups of cut baby corn
  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in curry paste, minced garlic, and minced ginger; cook and stir for 2 minutes. Add in cubed chicken, and cook until the pieces turn white on the outside, about 3 minutes.
  2. Mix in peanut butter, brown sugar, and tamarind paste, stirring to combine. Add potatoes, carrots, baby corn, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the potatoes are tender and the chicken pieces are no longer pink in the center, about 20 minutes.
  3. Add lime juice and cook for an additional 5 minutes before serving.
